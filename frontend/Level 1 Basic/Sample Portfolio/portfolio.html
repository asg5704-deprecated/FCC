While serving in the Army, my job didn’t specifically deal with information technology. I was a systems maintainer/administrator for the Army’s air defense equipment. I have worked very closely with defense companies like Northrop Grumman, Raytheon, BAE Systems, and Lockheed Martin. Helping to develop standard operating procedures and ensuring quality assurances were up to Army standards. The Army gave me the opportunity to learn more about myself while allowing me to earn my educational benefit that no other job could provide.
